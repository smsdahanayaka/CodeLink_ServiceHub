   - **Email:** your@email.com

## Step 3: Add Your First Product (2 minutes)

Before creating warranty cards, you need products in the system.

1. Click **Products** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Product** button
3. Fill in:
   ```
   Product Name:     Smart TV 55"
   Model Number:     TV-55-4K
   Warranty Period:  12 months
   ```
4. Click **Create**

âœ… **Done!** Your first product is added.

---

## Step 4: Add a Shop (2 minutes)

Shops are your dealers or retail points.

1. Click **Shops** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Shop** button
3. Fill in:
   ```
   Shop Name:    ABC Electronics
   Phone:        9876543210
   City:         Mumbai
   ```
4. Click **Create**

âœ… **Done!** Your first shop is registered.

---

## Step 5: Create a Warranty Card (2 minutes)

Now register a product warranty for a customer.

1. Click **Warranty Cards** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Warranty Card** button
3. Fill in:
   ```
   Product:        Smart TV 55" (select from dropdown)
   Shop:           ABC Electronics (select from dropdown)
   Serial Number:  SN-2024-001234
   Purchase Date:  Today's date

   Customer (New):
   - Name:  John Doe
   - Phone: 9876543210
   ```
4. Click **Create**

âœ… **Done!** Warranty card created with a new customer.

---

## Step 6: Create Your First Claim (2 minutes)

When a customer reports an issue, create a claim.

1. Click **Claims** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Claim** button
3. Search for the warranty card (by serial number: SN-2024-001234)
4. Fill in:
   ```
   Issue Description:  Screen flickering when turned on
   Priority:           Medium
   Reported By:        Customer
   ```
5. Click **Create Claim**

âœ… **Congratulations!** You've created your first warranty claim.

## What's Next?

### Process the Claim

1. Open the claim you just created
2. Click on the current workflow step
3. Fill in any required information
4. Click **Complete Step** to move forward
5. If prompted, select the user for the next step

### Add Sub-Tasks (Optional)

Break down complex steps into smaller tasks:

1. Open a claim with an active workflow step
2. Find the "Sub-Tasks" section in the step card
3. Click **Add** to create a sub-task
4. Assign it to a team member
5. Complete all sub-tasks before finishing the step

### Schedule a Pickup (Optional)

If you need to collect the product:

1. Go to **Logistics** â†’ **Pickups**
2. Click **Schedule Pickup**
3. Select the claim
4. Set date and time
5. Click **Schedule**

### View Your Tasks

1. Click **My Tasks** in the sidebar
2. See all claims assigned to you
3. Process them in priority order

## New Features Highlight

### Sub-Tasks
Break down workflow steps into smaller, assignable tasks:
- Add sub-tasks to the current step
- Assign to team members
- Track progress with completion percentage
- Steps can't complete until sub-tasks are done

### Next User Selection
Choose who handles the next step:
- Appears when completing a step
- Shows eligible users with workload
- Search and select the right person
- Pre-assign users when creating claims
